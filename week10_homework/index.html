Global Missing Migrants

Every year there are thousands of incidents where migrants go missing all around the world, with most cases involve deaths due to various reasons.
This visualization explores the information surrounding all cases reported from 2014 to 2023.

Location

The following map shows where all cases from 2014 to 2023 took place around the world.
It can be seen that most incidents are centered around the US-Mexico border, North border of Africa and Southest Asia.

By Category / Gender

The adjacent chart shows the total number of Male, Female and Children victims (migrants) who are involved in incidents each year.

Move the slider to view data for year(s) 2014 to 2023 (current).
